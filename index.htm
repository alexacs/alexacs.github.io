I am currently a Postdoctoral Fellow at the American Academy of Arts and Sciences. I received my PhD from the Department of Politics at Princeton, where I was affiliated with the Center for the Study of Democratic Politics and the Program in Law and Public Affairs.

My research focuses on the political economy of regulation and the challenges of policymaking in modern democracies. I draw upon insights from the fields of positive political theory, organizational economics, administrative law and public administration.
